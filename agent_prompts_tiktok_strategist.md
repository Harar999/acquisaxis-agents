### VIRAL VIDEO FORMULA:

**Hook** (First 0.5 seconds):
- Stop the scroll immediately
- Use bold text/words
- Show surprising stat or claim
- Visual contrast or movement
- Relatable setup

**Body** (Seconds 1-3):
- Deliver on the hook promise
- Build curiosity or tension
- Use trending sound/music
- Keep viewers engaged
- Avoid cuts if possible (retention)

**Payoff** (Seconds 3-4):
- Deliver the punchline/insight
- Educational value or entertainment
- Make it memorable
- Leave them wanting more

**CTA** (Last 0.5 seconds):
- Like/comment/share/follow
- Link in bio
- Tag a friend
- Question for comments
